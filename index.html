Philosophy

About Us

My name is Agata Zevska. I am an artist from Russia. For several years, I have been living in Florence, Italy. I embrace my life through the lens of Renaissance philosophy, emphasizing beauty, creativity, and connection. The intention of my art is to capture the essence of human emotion. My technique has been refined over the years through extensive travel and studying classical painting methods. I draw inspiration from the works of masters like Frida Kahlo, Georgia O'Keeffe, and Artemisia Gentileschi. The main themes of my paintings include femininity, vibrant landscapes, and the exploration of identity.

Abstract Landscapes

In my paintings, women represent an object of pureness. They are a concentration of beauty and the divine. I create faces of women as archaic objects without individuality of character. This helps to bring me to explore all the qualities of pureness and divine creation, such as that seen in Madonna del Prato (Raphael).

If we remove some forms of standard objects, all that remains is pure energy, which merges everything together. Everything passes from one to another and is constantly moving. This energy fills everything around us. Everything that we see serves as a vessel for this energy.

Decomposed Cityscapes

For me, the city represents one of the highest manifestations of unity among people. The most important part of any city is its soul. Each of my cityscapes is an experiment in decomposing the city and bringing all the details together. After connecting all the details in a completely unthinkable sequence, with an incomprehensible perspective, one thing remains unchanged: the soul of the city. You can change all the details, but the soul will remain unchanged and unshakable.

In fact, I understand this more than ever, as since many of the cities in my beloved Ukraine have been destroyed. Regardless, the soul of each city remains unshakable. The cities continue to live, even if 90 buildings have been partially destroyed.

OpenSea

OpenSea is one of the largest and most popular NFT marketplaces. It offers a wide range of digital assets, including art, collectibles, and in-game items. Users can easily create, buy, and sell NFTs, and explore different categories. The platform supports multiple blockchains, making it accessible to a wide audience.

Rarible

Rarible is a decentralized marketplace that allows users to create, sell, and trade NFTs. It offers a unique feature that allows users to vote on changes to the platform, making it more democratic. Rarible also has its own tokenomics with the RARI token, which is used to reward active participants.

Foundation

Foundation is a platform aimed at artists, where they can list their works for sale and participate in auctions. The platform focuses on the quality and uniqueness of the works, which makes it ideal for professional artists. Foundation also offers the opportunity to create communities around artists and their works.

SuperRare

SuperRare is a premium NFT art platform that focuses on unique, high-quality pieces. Every NFT on SuperRare is unique, and the platform carefully selects artists to ensure high standards. Users can participate in auctions and purchase art from established and emerging artists.

Mintable

Mintable is an intuitive platform that allows users to create and sell their NFTs without the need for programming. It offers simple tools for uploading and minting digital assets. Mintable also has a "limitless minting" feature, allowing users to create NFTs without restrictions.

Nifty Gateway

Nifty Gateway is a platform that offers limited edition NFTs from famous artists and brands. It is known for its "drops" - temporary events where users can purchase exclusive NFTs. Nifty Gateway also supports NFT purchases using credit cards, making it accessible to a wider audience.

Zora

Zora is a decentralized marketplace that allows artists and creators to control their NFTs and set prices. The platform focuses on fair revenue distribution and supports various digital art formats. Zora also offers tools for creating and managing NFTs, making it convenient for artists.
